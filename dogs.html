Fun Facts About Samoyeds

  • Samoyeds are Very Happy Dogs
  • Samoyeds Were Originally Bred in Siberia
  • Samoyeds Smile to Prevent Icicles from Damaging Their Mouths
  • Samoyeds Can Survive Below-Freezing Temperatures
  • Samoyeds Don’t Carry a “Doggy” Scent
  • Samoyeds are Closely Related to Wolves
